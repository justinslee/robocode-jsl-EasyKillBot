Movement:

EasyKillBot moves to center of the playing field then moves in a circle of a chosen size.

Targeting:

EasyKillBot scans for a robot. Once it has locked on to a robot it attempts to remain locked until the robot either leaves the range of the radar.

Firing:

EasyKillBot fires more powerful bullets the closer it is to its target. It fires only when it has a lock on the target tank.

Vulnerabilities:

EasyKillBot fires constantly at last known enemy position. It is weak against good evasive bots or bots that never return to the same line of sight of the bullets. This weakness can be fixed with a targeting algorithm. Also it does not track until it moves to its goal initially.
